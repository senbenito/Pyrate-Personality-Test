Me hearty, what is this Pyrate Personality Test?

The Pyrate Personality Test is loosely based on the MBTI (Myers-Briggs Type Indicator), and was created by renowned sociologist, and pirate aficianado, Dr. Jerome Mayfield in 2002. Pivoting off the foundational principles of Carl Jung and, of course, Katharine Cook Briggs and her daughter, Isabel Briggs Myers, Dr. Mayfield sought to condense the 16 MBTI Types into a more useable - and relatable - format.

Who be the Pyrate Personalities?
